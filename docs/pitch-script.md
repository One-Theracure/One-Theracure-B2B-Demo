# One TheraCure — 10-Minute Pitch Script

> **Audience:** Hospital administrators, multi-clinic owners, and healthcare investors in India.
> **Format:** A live, guided demo. The presenter clicks through the in-app **"Take the tour"** button on the dashboard while reading from this script. The script and the in-app tour are kept in lockstep — each in-app step has a one-line caption that matches a section here.
> **Goal:** End the meeting with the prospect saying "show me the pricing page" or "let's set up a pilot".

---

## How to use this script

1. Open the deployed app at the public URL (see the project README) and sign in with the demo account.
2. Land on the **Dashboard**. Click **"Take the tour"** in the Quick Actions card.
3. Read the section below that matches the on-screen step. The bolded line is the takeaway you want the prospect to remember.
4. Stay on time — the budget per stop is enforced. If a question comes up, defer to the FAQ block at the end and keep moving.
5. End on the multilingual After-Visit Summary. That's the emotional moment: "this is what the patient walks home with".

Total budget: **10:00 minutes** of speaking + ~2 minutes for live Q&A at the end.

## 2:30 — 4:00 · Stop 2 — AI Safety Alert + Accepted Swap (1:30)

**On screen:** Tour step 3. A safety alert fires when a medication is added; doctor accepts a safer alternative.

> "Second stop: safety. When the doctor adds a medication to the plan, our safety engine runs in the background — interactions, allergies, dose ceilings, pregnancy categories, and patient-specific risk factors from the chart.

The override (or accept) is logged with the doctor's name and a timestamp.

## FAQ — Anticipated Objections

Use these only when asked. Do not pre-empt them in the script.

### "How is patient data secured?"

- All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
- Role-based access control — front desk, doctor, admin each see only what they need.
- Every AI output is logged with the user, timestamp, and source inputs (full audit trail).
- We're HIPAA-aligned and ABDM-compliant. We're happy to walk through our DPDP Act readiness on a follow-up call with your CISO.

### "How does this scale to 50 / 100 / 500 clinics?"

- The platform is multi-tenant from day one — every clinic is its own organisation with its own users, data, and branding.
- Hosted on Replit's autoscale infrastructure: usage-based, no idle cost.
- Onboarding a new clinic is a self-serve flow — typically under 30 minutes from signup to first visit.

### "Is the ABDM integration real or simulated?"

- The ABDM data flow you saw in the demo is simulated against a sample patient (Mrs. Fatima Sheikh) so we can show you the experience end-to-end in a meeting.
- The real ABDM sandbox integration is on our short-term roadmap — we're already approved as a Health Information User (HIU). The handoff UX you saw will not change; only the data source moves from simulated to live.

### "How much does it cost?"

- Per-doctor monthly pricing, with volume discounts for multi-clinic groups.
- The two-week pilot is free.
- We can share a pricing sheet on the follow-up call once we've scoped your specialty mix and clinic count.

### "Is the AI accurate? What happens if it gets something wrong?"

- Every AI output is presented as a **draft** that requires explicit clinician acceptance. Nothing is auto-applied to a patient record without a human in the loop.
- The doctor can edit any field before finalising. The original AI suggestion and the final clinician-approved version are both stored, so we have a full provenance trail.
- Our safety engine is rule-based (drug interactions, allergies, dose ceilings) — not generative — so safety alerts are deterministic and auditable.

### "Do doctors actually use it, or is it shelfware?"

- Adoption metric we care about: % of visits where the scribe was started. In our pilots that number is north of 80% by week two.
- The doctor-facing UI is built around one rule: *one click closer to going home on time*. Anything that doesn't make the visit faster gets cut.

### "What about the patient — do they need to download anything?"

- No. The After-Visit Summary is a printed sheet plus a QR they can scan with the camera app on any phone. No login, no app install, no friction.
